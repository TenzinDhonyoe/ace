# Exam structure

## Course
CHEM250 — Organic Chemistry I

## Format
- ~25 MCQs, heavy mechanism emphasis
- Students draw curly-arrow mechanisms for 3-4 reactions

## Sections to cover
1. Functional groups & nomenclature
2. SN1 vs SN2 substitution
3. E1 vs E2 elimination
4. Alkene addition reactions
