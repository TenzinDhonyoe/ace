# Exam structure

## Course
BIOL210 — Intro Biochemistry

## Format
- ~40 MCQs + 3 short-answer pathway/mechanism questions
- Emphasis on enzyme kinetics and metabolism

## Sections to cover
1. Amino acids & protein structure
2. Enzyme kinetics (Michaelis-Menten, inhibition types)
3. Glycolysis & the TCA cycle
4. Oxidative phosphorylation & ATP synthesis
