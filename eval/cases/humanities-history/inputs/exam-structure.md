# Exam structure

## Course
HIST310 — 20th Century Europe

## Format
- ~15 MCQs on events, dates, actors
- 2 essay-prep questions: causation, historiographical debates

## Sections to cover
1. Origins of WWI (alliance system, July Crisis)
2. Interwar period (Versailles, Weimar collapse, rise of fascism)
3. WWII in Europe (Eastern Front, Holocaust, liberation)
4. Cold War beginnings (Yalta, Marshall Plan, Berlin blockade)
